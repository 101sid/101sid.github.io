 About Us

We endeavour to use the best ingredients we can source, where possible we know where our fresh product is sourced. Our meals are balanced and nutritional, we fry or saute in minimal oil and our meats are trimmed of any fats before cooking. This includes previously regarded high fat items such as burgers. There are no animal oils used in any food preparations. Should you have any specific cooking method requirements please make yourself known to us.
